Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Social Video Poster is a desktop automation tool used to upload user-provided video content to social media accounts that the user owns or controls.

Information We Process

We may process the following information when the app is used:

  • TikTok authorization tokens granted by the user.
  • Basic account identifiers required to complete uploads.
  • Video files and associated captions supplied by the user.
  • Operational logs related to upload status and scheduling.

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate the user with TikTok.
  • To upload videos selected by the user to the user's own TikTok account.
  • To maintain scheduling state and prevent duplicate uploads.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data is shared only with service providers required to perform the requested upload flow, such as TikTok APIs.

Data Retention

Authorization tokens and upload state may be stored locally for as long as needed to support the scheduling and upload functionality, unless deleted by the user.

User Rights

Users may revoke TikTok access through TikTok account settings or by removing locally stored token files and application state.
