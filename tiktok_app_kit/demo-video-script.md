# Demo Video Script

Target length:
60 to 120 seconds

Suggested script:

1. Show the landing page of Social Video Poster.
2. Show the privacy policy and terms pages briefly.
3. Show the local folder containing original sample videos.
4. Open the app or terminal workflow for Social Video Poster.
5. Start TikTok authorization for one account.
6. Approve the requested TikTok permission.
7. Return to the app and show the account is connected.
8. Show the scheduler/config that selects one video from the local folder.
9. Start the upload.
10. Show the resulting post confirmation or private visibility status in TikTok.

Voiceover example:
"This app helps creators upload their own local videos to their own TikTok accounts after explicit authorization. The user connects their TikTok account, chooses or schedules original content, and the app uploads that content using TikTok's official Content Posting API."
